For today’s Did You Know we will look at Fruit Punch Fun Facts.

Usually fruit juices are mixed together while making fruit punch. This is a very simple and easy recipe of fruit punch where I have used fresh Orange juice, Pineapple juice and lemon juice. The choice of juices can be yours, but do keep in mind that on mixing they should taste good. So you can just mix the juices in small quantities using a certain proportion. Mix and then check the taste. If the taste is good, then you can safely mix the same juices in large quantities in the same proportion.
